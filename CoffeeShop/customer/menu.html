Our speciality

Cafe Americano

A Café Americano is a robust coffee drink that combines espresso with hot water, resulting in a smooth, bold taste. It's less intense than a straight shot but more pronounced than a standard brewed coffee, showcasing the pure, rich flavors of espresso.

Lasangna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel (white sauce), and cheese, all baked to perfection. This hearty and flavorful dish is a favorite comfort food, known for its rich, savory taste and satisfying texture.

Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern street food made with marinated meat that is slow-cooked on a vertical rotisserie. Thin slices of the cooked meat are shaved off and typically served in a warm pita or flatbread, pickles, and a variety of flavorful sauces.
